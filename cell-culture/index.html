Cell Culture

How to Play

You run a petri dish. The cells divide on their own and steadily produce "biomass" (BM). The core loop is simple: cells generate biomass → spend biomass on more cells and upgrades → your rate rises → you generate even more biomass — a snowball that keeps growing.

Getting started: you begin with zero biomass, so click the dish to earn your first coins by hand. Once you have 15 BM, buy your first E. coli. From then on income accumulates automatically, and the share coming from clicks naturally shrinks as you scale — that is exactly the appeal of an idle game: it earns while you are away, and rewards you when you return.

Prestige: once your lifetime biomass this run reaches one billion (1e9), the "Prestige" option unlocks. Prestiging wipes your biomass, all cells, and all upgrades, but grants a permanent currency, "Medium" (ML). Each point of Medium adds +2% to your global output, kept forever and stacking across prestiges. Timing your prestige well lets you run faster and further next time.

Controls

· Click the dish: harvest biomass by hand — essential in the early game.

· Click a strain on the right: buy one cell of that strain; the cost rises with how many you own.

· Hold Shift and click a strain: buy up to 10 at once.

· Upgrades: buy milestone upgrades for a single strain (double its output), or global upgrades.

· Prestige / Stats / Settings: below the strain list; Settings lets you export, import, or clear your save.

Progress auto-saves locally (localStorage) every 10 seconds. Close the page and return, and offline income is settled (50% efficiency, capped at 12 hours).
