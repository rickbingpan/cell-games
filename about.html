About Cell Games

Cell Games is an independent hobby project — a small collection of free browser games united by a single theme: the cell. It is built and maintained by one developer with a background in bioinformatics, which is where the biology flavor and the recurring cast of cells, strains, and lab equipment come from.

What makes these games different

  • Pure front-end. Everything runs in your browser. There is nothing to install and no account to create.
  • Play instantly. Open a game and start — on desktop or mobile.
  • Your data stays with you. Progress and high scores are saved locally in your own browser, not on our servers.

The games

Cell Dodge is a bullet-hell action game about weaving a single cell through waves of projectiles. Cell Culture is an idle incremental game where a petri dish of cells grows and earns on its own. Cell Mimic is a top-down hide-and-seek game where a cell disguises itself as lab equipment to evade an AI inspector. The three share a light continuity — the hero of Cell Mimic is the cell that escaped the dish in Cell Culture.

Feedback

These games are actively tinkered with, and player feedback genuinely shapes them. If you hit a bug, have a balance suggestion, or just want to say hello, please visit the Contact page.
