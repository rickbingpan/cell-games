About Cell Games

Cell Games is a small, independent collection of browser games built around a single theme: the cell. Every title is pure front-end — nothing to download, no account to create. Open a game in your desktop or mobile browser and play right away. Your progress is saved locally in your own browser.

🕹️ Cell Dodge — bullet-hell action

Steer a lone cell through dense waves of projectiles. Weave between shots, collect drops and upgrades, and survive to the boss. It rewards quick reactions and clean movement.

🧫 Cell Culture — idle incremental

Manage a petri dish. Cells divide and generate biomass automatically; spend it to buy upgrades and unlock eight strains, from E. coli to a synthetic cell, then prestige for permanent bonuses. It even accrues income while the tab is closed.

🔍 Cell Mimic — top-down hide-and-seek

A cell that slipped out of the culture flask disguises itself as lab glassware to blend in among identical props and dodge an AI inspector — stay still to stay safe, move and risk suspicion. Play as the hider to outlast the timer, or switch to the seeker mode and root out the disguised cells yourself.

All three games share one continuity: the star of Cell Mimic is the very cell that escaped the petri dish in Cell Culture.
