Cell Mimic

How to Play

A cell that escaped its petri dish has wandered onto a lab bench cluttered with equipment. To avoid being cleaned up by the inspector, it has learned one trick — mimicry: turn into a nearby piece of lab equipment and sit perfectly still among identical ones.

All the fun comes from one tension: stay still to stay safe, move and risk everything. Disguised and motionless, you are just another prop; but the moment you move inside the inspector's vision cone, a "prop" that walks looks suspicious, and it will come over to check. Get caught and you are out.

There are two modes: Mimic & Hide — you are the hider; outlast the timer to clear the level, and each level gets harder; and Inspector Hunt — you become the seeker, rooting out the disguised cells from a crowd of props.

Controls

· Desktop (Mimic & Hide): move with W A S D or the arrow keys, Space to mimic/release, Esc to pause.

· Mobile (Mimic & Hide): press and drag on the left side of the stage to bring up a virtual joystick; tap the Mimic button at the bottom-right to transform.

· Inspector Hunt (both): click / tap a prop to "inspect" it.

Tip: you can only mimic a prop when you are close to it; once disguised, staying still among a cluster of the same prop is the safest.

Equipment Field Guide

These are the six props you can mimic. Each one is a real piece of laboratory equipment.

Petri Dish dish
A shallow round dish used to culture microbial colonies — the most common vessel in a lab.
Test Tube glass
A slim glass tube for holding, mixing, and heating small liquid samples.
Beaker glassware
A wide graduated cup for preparing solutions and rough liquid measurement.
Centrifuge Tube tube
A cone-bottomed tube that, in a centrifuge, separates a sample's components by density.
Alcohol Lamp burner
A small alcohol-fueled lamp used for heating and flame sterilization.
Culture Flask flask
A round-bellied, narrow-necked flask for growing cells in liquid medium.

Tips & Strategy

  • After being seen moving, don't freeze on the spot — the inspector walks to the "suspicious point" to check. Use the 0.6-second wind-up while it raises its hand to slip out of view and re-mimic elsewhere.
  • Mimic where lots of the same prop are clustered; it is hard for a check to land precisely on you.
  • The alcohol lamp's flame always flickers — natural cover. The inspector only reacts to overall movement, never to a prop's built-in animation.
  • In Inspector Hunt, the prop that quietly twitches or shifts is almost always a disguised cell.

Universe

Cell Mimic shares a cell universe with Cell Dodge (bullet-hell) and Cell Culture (idle) — and its hero is the very cell that slipped out of the petri dish in Cell Culture.
