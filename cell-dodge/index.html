How to Play

You control a single cell adrift in a hostile field. Enemies fill the screen with projectiles, and your only job is to survive. Weave through the gaps, grab drops to power up, and push deeper until you face the boss. Getting hit costs you — clean movement and good positioning are everything.

Controls. On desktop, move with the W A S D or arrow keys. On a touch screen, drag anywhere to steer your cell. The cell follows your input continuously, so small, deliberate movements beat frantic ones.

Tip: don't chase power-ups into tight spaces. Survival first, greed second.

About the Game

Cell Dodge is a bullet-hell (danmaku) action game and the first title in the Cell Games collection. It is built with the Godot engine and runs entirely in your browser — no download, no sign-up. Your best runs are tracked locally on your device.

Part of a shared cell universe alongside Cell Culture and Cell Mimic.
